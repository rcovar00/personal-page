Cognitive Load Evaluation in a Educational Videogame

  • Category: UX Research

This study was conducted to evaluate cognitive load of an educational video game oriented to teach and reinforce the concept of fractions. A total of 18 participants played two difficulty levels of Refraction 2 video game. The results show significant differences in pupil size and peak dilation between levels.
